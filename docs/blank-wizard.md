# Blank / Erase Disc Wizard

Guided step-by-step workflow for erasing rewritable discs or formatting blank media.

---

## Step 1: Select Drive

Choose the optical drive containing the rewritable disc. The wizard shows:

- Drive model and firmware
- Media type detected in the drive
- Compatibility warnings (e.g., if the disc is write-once and cannot be erased)

Click **Next** to proceed.

---

## Step 2: Erase Options

### Quick Erase

Erases the Table of Contents (TOC) and PMA only. Fast (seconds), but old data remains physically on the disc.

**Compatible:** CD-RW, DVD-RW, DVD+RW, DVD-RAM, BD-RE, HD DVD-RW

### Full Erase

Writes zeros to the entire disc surface. Complete wipe but slow (15–60+ minutes).

**Compatible:** Same as Quick Erase

> **Note:** BD-R and DVD±R are write-once — they can be formatted but not erased.

---

## Step 3: Execute

The wizard shows:
- Progress bar (indeterminate for full erase)
- Estimated time remaining
- Status messages
- Detailed operation log

Click **Cancel** to abort at any time.
