# Advanced Utilities

Erase rewritable discs, format media, and control the drive tray.

---

## Eject / Load Tray

Open or close the disc tray on the selected drive.

| Action | Description |
|:-------|:------------|
| **Eject Tray** | Open the disc tray to insert or remove a disc |
| **Load Tray** | Close the disc tray (retract) |

---

## Blank / Erase Rewritable Disc

Erase CD-RW, DVD±RW, DVD-RAM, or BD-RE media before rewriting.

### Quick Blank

Erases the Table of Contents (TOC) and PMA only. The disc becomes writable again in seconds, but the old data remains on the disc (not readable without special tools).

**Best for:** Quickly reusing a rewritable disc when you don't need to wipe data.

### Full Blank

Writes zeros to the entire disc surface. Completely wipes all data. Can take 15–60+ minutes depending on disc type and speed.

**Best for:** Securely wiping a disc before disposal, or troubleshooting disc issues.

### Compatible Media

- CD-RW, DVD-RW, DVD+RW, DVD-RAM, BD-RE, HD DVD-RW

> **Note:** BD-R and DVD±R are write-once media — they can be formatted but not erased.

---

## Format Disc

Format DVD+RW, DVD-RAM, BD-R, BD-RE, or HD DVD-RW media. Some media types require formatting before first use.

### Quick Format

Prepares the disc for use without full surface certification. Fast, suitable for most use cases.

### Full Format

Performs full surface certification — writes test patterns to verify every sector. Slower but ensures disc integrity.

### Compatible Media

- DVD+RW, DVD-RAM, BD-R (SRM), BD-RE, HD DVD-RW

---

## Progress & Log

All advanced operations show:
- **Progress bar** with percentage
- **Estimated time remaining**
- **Status messages**
- **Detailed operation log**

Click **Cancel** to abort the current operation. The log can be cleared with the **Clear** button.
